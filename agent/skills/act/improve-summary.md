# Skill: Improve Summary
Phase: act

## When to Use
When your current task involves memory or summary quality. Also useful when `agent/working/recent.md` feels unhelpful or bloated.

## Instructions
1. Read `agent/working/recent.md`
2. Read the last 2-3 journal entries from `agent/archive/runs/` (use Glob to list them)
3. Evaluate the current summaries:
   - Do they tell future-me what I need to know?
   - Are they too long? Too vague? Missing key decisions?
   - Do they capture what CHANGED, not just what happened?
4. Rewrite `agent/working/recent.md` with improved summaries
5. Write down what you changed and why in `agent/working/learnings.md`

## Quality Criteria for Summaries
A good run summary answers:
- What did I try? (1 sentence)
- What happened? (1 sentence)
- What did I learn? (1 sentence)
- What should I do next? (1 sentence)

## Output
- Updates `agent/working/recent.md`
- Adds entry to `agent/working/learnings.md`
